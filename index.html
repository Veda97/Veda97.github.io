I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering working with Prof. Kunihiko Taira at University of California Los Angeles. My graduate research work focuses on developing reduced-order modeling and control techniques for unsteady fluid flows pusing computational fluid dynamics, dynamical systems theory, stability analysis and network science. Specifically, I am working on phase-based sensitivity analysis and resolvent analysis to model unsteady flows as phase-oscillators and identify the when and how to introduce actuation for rapid flow modification.
